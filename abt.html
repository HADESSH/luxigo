Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Luxigo began as a small startup with a big vision: to revolutionize the digital landscape through innovative solutions and exceptional service. What started as a team of three passionate developers has grown into a global company with over 200 employees across five countries.

Our journey has been marked by relentless pursuit of excellence, continuous learning, and a commitment to delivering value that exceeds expectations. We've evolved from a web development agency to a full-service digital transformation partner for businesses worldwide.
